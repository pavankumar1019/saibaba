About Temple

Inagurated on 25/10/2020

Om Shri Shirdi Sai Baba is a Spritual Master and God of All Religions. His Great Teachings will inform all are Equal. Smt.Dasamma Munithimmaiah has started Constructing a Temple "Shirdi Sai Baba" (DM.Shirdi Sai Baba Temple Trust) in the year 2019 for all the Devotees.Constructing a Temple is an impossible work,to make this work possible devotees as raise a hand By Donating to the temple.(With the Divine Blessings of baba,if you want to donate please donate by approching the temple or donate through online Click Here ). Smt.Dasamma Munithimmaiah son M.Munimarappa taken all the role in Developing the temple .The Temple was beyond the Scope of the various Vedanta centers that existed that time. The temple management has brought the priests who were masterd in vedas and highly skilled. The temple Campus is very peace with good environment for all the devotees and visitors. Idle's sculpted in the temple is very Beautiful and Each idle define a great Infromation about the Ancient hindu Sulpture
